Sunflower

They are one of the most popular flower types!
Symbol of harvest and bounty and is visually beautiful.
They make great gifts and look wonderful in bouquets.

Daisy

They belong to one of the largest known
plant families and symbolized innocence.

Lavender

They are sweet herbs that provide soothing fragrances
and beauty all together in floral packages.
They also help relieve stress and anxiety to bring about sleep.
